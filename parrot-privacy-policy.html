Data Collection and Use:

Parrot is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect any personal data from you. This means we do not track your usage, collect analytics, or transmit any information to third-party servers. The App's sole function is to record audio, save it locally on your device's storage and play it back to you.

Any sharing of recorded files is handled exclusively by the iOS operating system and is subject to your device's privacy settings.
